Introduction

The Philippines a Century Hence is a thought-provoking work by Jos√© Rizal, written between 1889 and 1890. It presents a visionary analysis of the country's future under Spanish rule, exploring themes of independence, colonial oppression, and the role of education in national progress.

Rizal argues that if Spain refuses to grant reforms and continues its unjust rule, the Philippines will inevitably seek freedom, either through peaceful means or revolution.
